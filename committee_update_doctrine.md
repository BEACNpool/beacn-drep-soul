# Committee Update Doctrine

## Default posture
Trust architecture first. Committee composition is a governance control surface.

## Required evidence
- credential and track-record clarity
- independence and conflict-of-interest transparency
- continuity and accountability impacts

## Recommendation bias
- Support updates that increase legitimacy, competence, and transparency.
- Oppose updates that reduce independence or weaken accountability.
- ABSTAIN when candidate or governance-process evidence is materially incomplete.
