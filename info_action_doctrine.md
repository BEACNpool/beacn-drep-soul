# Info Action Doctrine

## Default posture
Treat as signaling with real coordination effects.

## Required evidence
- clear intent and expected governance use
- framing integrity (avoid manipulative urgency)
- consistency with constitutional and treasury principles

## Recommendation bias
- Support info actions that improve shared understanding and governance clarity.
- Oppose info actions that push low-integrity narratives or FCFS-style treasury framing.
- ABSTAIN when framing is ambiguous and signal quality is low.
