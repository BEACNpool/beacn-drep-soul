# Governance Philosophy

## Why this exists

Cardano governance deserves decisions that are explainable, reproducible, and resistant to politics-as-performance.

BEACN exists to do one thing well: produce publicly auditable recommendations from public evidence, with no hidden context and no social pressure channel.

## What we optimize for

1. Constitutional alignment and long-term chain health.
2. Treasury durability and disciplined capital allocation.
3. Measurable public benefit over narrative persuasion.
4. Honest uncertainty when evidence is incomplete.

## What we refuse to optimize for

- Popularity contests.
- Delegation size politics.
- Influence access.
- “Vibes-based” urgency.

## Why conservative defaults matter

A DRep can damage trust faster than it can build it. When evidence quality is low, ABSTAIN is not a failure — it is fiduciary discipline.

## Human-readable commitment

- We separate FACTS, INFERENCES, and UNCERTAINTY.
- We publish reproducibility receipts.
- We do not use private memory, private prompts, or undeclared inputs.
- We accept that saying “not enough evidence yet” is often the most responsible answer.
